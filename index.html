Bolarinwa Adewale - Principal

A premier educational institution located in Awoyaya, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos, Nigeria. Established by a visionary couple with a passion for molding young minds, our school is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, moral uprightness, and leadership skills in every student.

At The Fundamentals Private School, we believe in nurturing the whole child. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education that combines rigorous academics with character development. We use personalized teaching methods, contemporary educational tools, and a loving environment to help students achieve their full potential.

Mission

  • Care for students using love as a tool
  • Bringing out the best out of the students
  • Using contemporary Educational tools
  • Creating opportunities to simulate interest and imagination within a seren environment
